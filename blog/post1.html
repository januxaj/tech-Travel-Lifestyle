✈️ How I Traveled to Japan on a Student Budget

Published: July 2025

Planning Ahead

I started preparing 3 months before the trip. I tracked flight prices using Skyscanner and booked when the rates dropped. I also researched the best hostels and transport passes.

Transportation

The Japan Rail Pass was a lifesaver. For a flat fee, I could ride unlimited bullet trains. I also used bikes in Kyoto and budget buses in Tokyo.

Accommodation

  • Capsule Hotels: Cheap, clean, and unique.
  • HostelWorld: Great for comparing prices and reviews.
  • Couchsurfing: Stayed with a local in Osaka — amazing cultural experience!

Food & Culture

I ate at 7-Eleven and family-run ramen shops. Meals were affordable and delicious. I also visited free museums, temples, and walked through scenic streets daily.

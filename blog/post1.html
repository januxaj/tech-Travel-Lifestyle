✈️ How I Traveled to Japan on a Student Budget

Posted on: July 2025

Japan is often considered a pricey travel destination, but with careful planning, I made it work on a student budget. Here’s how I did it:

  • Flight Deals: I booked my ticket three months early using Skyscanner and saved over 30%.
  • Accommodation: I stayed in capsule hotels and used Couchsurfing and HostelWorld for budget options.
  • Transportation: The Japan Rail Pass helped me explore the country affordably.
  • Food: I enjoyed cheap, quality meals at local ramen shops and convenience stores.

This trip taught me that traveling doesn’t require a fortune — just resourcefulness and planning.
